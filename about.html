About me

I'm Luca Teodori, currently a physics student. I am from Urbino, Marche, and I am studying physics at the University of Padova, working on my master thesis.

I did my bachelor at the University of Pavia, and I was accepted at the Volta college, where I also did an activity of tutorship on geometry and linear algebra for first year mathematicians, physicists and engineers (here you can find some notes I did for that occasion). The main reason that made me choose physics were topics related to the birth and evolution of the universe, so I decided to move in Padova for my master degree; this is due to the fact that in Pavia there was no cosmology group and hence no cosmology exams, differently from Padova.
