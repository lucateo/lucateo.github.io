Il Teodori: the project

In studying physics I found always fascinating how the various topics of this subject are correlated, forming a great unitary structure. But due to this feature I found always hard to connect notation and above all the mathematics used in some courses (let be honest: in some courses maths is used, to tell it with a well known italian way of saying, "alla cazzo di cane"). So here's the idea: create a monumental didactic opera where all is laid out and nothing is left to the curious reader as a trivial exercise (there will be exercises of course, but there will be detailed solutions to all of them). There will be also all the mathematics with all the theory that is needed for a physicist, and there will be all the internal cross references to all the equations and all the topics.

But I cannot do this all on my own! I need your help to do this, if you want above in this site you can find my contacts. We can start with physics and maybe then from the physics background we can move to chemistry, then biology, then try to include all the scientific topics in a great, self contained, gigantic opera and the conquer the world, the galaxy and the whole universe!
